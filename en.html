Penzion u Klujů

A cozy guesthouse with a family atmosphere offers cheap accommodation right in the middle of beautiful nature. Suitable for both active and quiet holidays.

Why come to us?

Cozy guesthouse with family atmosphere, capacity 28 beds , offers cheap accommodation near Vír dam, right in the middle of beutiful nature. Suitable for both active and quiet holidays. Autumn in Vysočině is right time to peaceful vacation experience for those who are looking for peace and not overcrowded hotels.

In the vicinity of the guesthouse there are two lookout towers, castle ruins and also the well-known Pernštejn Castle.

There are many tourist routes, marked cycle paths and cycling trails for tourists and cyclists. In the village of Vír, you can rent a tennis court, play bowling or use a workout field. In winter, you can go skiing in nearby Olešnica, Karasín, Dalečín or Nové Město na Moravě, where several cross-country ski tracks are maintained in the winter.

In the pension, you can use a common room for sport and social activities, table tennis, Russian skittles, an outdoor playground and a grill. Free WiFi connection.
